Nuance Extension — Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Nuance ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension developed to provide AI-powered critical analysis of online news articles. This policy describes what data is collected, how it is processed, and your rights under applicable privacy law (including GDPR).

2. Data We Collect

The Extension collects the following data solely to provide its service:

  • Article text content: When an article page is detected, the visible text content of that page is extracted locally and sent to the Groq API for AI analysis. This text is not stored by us after the API response is returned.
  • Article URL and hostname: Used to cache the bias score badge for that domain locally on your device. Not transmitted to any server.
  • User preferences: Theme (light/dark/auto) and language selection are stored locally in chrome.storage.local on your device only.
  • Usage counter: A daily analysis count for free-tier limiting, stored locally on your device only.
  • Analysis history: Article titles, URLs, bias scores, and dates — stored locally on your device only. You can delete it at any time from the extension popup.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history beyond the current article session.
  • We do not collect personal identifiers (name, email, IP address) from free-tier users.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetise any data.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.
  • We do not run analytics on your reading behaviour.

4. Third-Party Services

Groq Cloud API: Article text (up to 3000 characters) is sent to Groq's API to generate the AI analysis. Groq's data processing is governed by their own Privacy Policy. We use Groq solely for inference; no data is retained by them for training without consent.

ExtensionPay: Pro subscription payments are processed by ExtensionPay. They collect your email address and payment information. See their Privacy Policy.

Google Favicon API: Source domain favicons are loaded from google.com/s2/favicons. This is a public API; no personal data is transmitted.

5. Data Retention

All locally stored data (preferences, history, usage counts) remains on your device and is cleared when you uninstall the extension. Article text sent to the Groq API is processed transiently and not retained by us.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR (if applicable), you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold. For free-tier users, all data is local to your device — you can clear it by uninstalling the extension or using the "Clear History" function in the popup. For Pro users, contact us to request account data deletion.

7. Security

The API key used to communicate with Groq is stored in a local env.js file and is not included in any public code repository. Communication with the Groq API is encrypted via HTTPS.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced through the extension update notes. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy enquiries: privacy@nuance.app

Nuance Pro — Subscription Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Scope

This supplementary policy applies exclusively to users of the Nuance Pro paid subscription tier ("Pro Users"), in addition to the Extension Privacy Policy above.

2. Data Collected for Pro Subscriptions

  • Email address: Collected by ExtensionPay at the time of purchase to identify your subscription and manage account access.
  • Payment information: Handled entirely by Stripe via ExtensionPay. We (Nuance) never see, store, or process your card details.
  • Subscription status: Your paid/unpaid status is retrieved from ExtensionPay servers each time you open the extension, to unlock Pro features. This is a read-only operation and involves transmitting only your installation's unique ExtensionPay identifier.

3. Payment Processing

All billing is handled by ExtensionPay (powered by Stripe). By subscribing, you agree to ExtensionPay's Terms of Service and Stripe's Privacy Policy. We do not store financial data of any kind.

4. What Pro Unlocks (and What It Does Not Change)

Upgrading to Pro does not change how the extension processes article content. The same privacy principles apply: article text is sent transiently to Groq, nothing is permanently stored server-side, and your reading data remains on your device.

5. Subscription Management

You may cancel your Pro subscription at any time by clicking "Manage Subscription" in the extension popup. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial months.

6. Account Deletion

To delete your Pro account and all associated data, email privacy@nuance.app. Your subscription will be cancelled and your email removed from ExtensionPay's records within 30 days.

7. Data Transfers

Your email address is stored by ExtensionPay, which operates within the United States. If you are based in the European Economic Area, this constitutes an international data transfer. ExtensionPay is compliant with applicable US-EU data transfer frameworks.

8. Contact

For subscription and billing enquiries: pro@nuance.app
