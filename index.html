Extension Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What we collect

Article text (up to 3,000 characters) is sent transiently to our proxy server, which forwards it to Groq's API for AI analysis. It is not stored by us after the response is returned.

User preferences (theme, language), usage counts, and reading history are stored locally on your device in chrome.storage.local only. They are never transmitted to any server.

2. What we never do

  • We do not collect personal identifiers from free-tier users
  • We do not sell, share, or monetise any data
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking pixels
  • We do not store article content after API responses
  • We do not collect your browsing history

3. Third-party services

Groq Cloud: Processes article text for AI inference. Governed by Groq's Privacy Policy. Data is not retained for training.

ExtensionPay / Stripe: Handles Pro payments. We never see card details. See ExtensionPay's Privacy Policy.

Google Favicon API: Public API to load source logos. No personal data transmitted.

4. Your rights (GDPR)

Clear your history anytime from the popup. Uninstalling the extension deletes all local data. For GDPR erasure requests: privacy@nuance.app

Pro Subscription Privacy Policy

Supplement to Extension Policy · January 2025

1. Data collected for Pro users

Email address: Collected by ExtensionPay at purchase to manage your subscription. Not stored by Nuance directly.

Payment data: Handled entirely by Stripe via ExtensionPay. We never see, store, or process card details.
